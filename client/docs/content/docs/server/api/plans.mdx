# Meal Plans & Pantry 

Endpoints for organizing diets, tracking stored food items, and managing shopping lists.

---

## 1. Meal Plans

### List Meal Plans

`GET /api/v1/meal-plans`

Retrieves historical meal plans generated for the user.

---

### Create Meal Plan

`POST /api/v1/meal-plans`

Saves a structured weekly or daily meal plan.

---

### Get Active Meal Plan

`GET /api/v1/meal-plans/active`

Returns the currently running schedule of meals and daily macro bounds.

---

### Get Meal Plan Details

`GET /api/v1/meal-plans/{id}`

Retrieves scheduled slots and recipe targets for a specific plan ID.

---

### Toggle Plan Active State

`PATCH /api/v1/meal-plans/{id}/status`

Toggles the active status of a meal plan.

---

### Update Meal Plan Item Consumed Status

`PATCH /api/v1/meal-plans/{id}/items/{item_id}/consumed`

Marks a scheduled meal item as consumed or skipped. Marks user consumed history by triggering a food log entry automatically.

---

## 2. Pantry Inventory

### List Pantry Inventory

`GET /api/v1/pantry`

Returns all food items currently stored in the user's kitchen pantry.

---

### Add Pantry Item

`POST /api/v1/pantry`

Add a food item to the user's digital pantry inventory.

---

### Update Pantry Item Quantity

`PATCH /api/v1/pantry/{id}`

Updates expiration date or quantity of a pantry item.

---

### Remove Pantry Item

`DELETE /api/v1/pantry/{id}`

Deletes an item from the digital pantry inventory.

---

## 3. Shopping Lists

### List Shopping Lists

`GET /api/v1/shopping-lists`

Retrieves all grocery lists generated.

---

### Create Shopping List

`POST /api/v1/shopping-lists`

Saves a custom or plan-generated shopping list.

---

### Get Shopping List Details

`GET /api/v1/shopping-lists/{id}`

Returns the shopping list items, estimated costs, and whether they have been acquired.

---

### Toggle Item Acquired Status

`PATCH /api/v1/shopping-lists/{id}/items/{item_id}`

Marks an item on the shopping list as acquired.
